Foundation Directors

Michael A. LaPorta II

Director, Asana Wellness Foundation

Michael A. LaPorta II brings a rare convergence of rigorous scientific training and heartfelt community commitment to the Asana Wellness Foundation. As a double major alumnus of UC Berkeley in Molecular & Cellular Biology and Public Health, Michael understands wellness not merely as an individual pursuit but as a measurable, population-level outcome shaped by equity, environment, and access.

Michael currently serves as a Research Associate at the Salk Institute, where he works under world-renowned cancer epidemiologist Dr. Susan Kaech. His research sits at the intersection of immunology, cancer biology, and population health — grounding the Foundation's programming in the latest evidence on how stress, inflammation, and lifestyle factors interact with long-term disease outcomes.

At the Asana Wellness Foundation, Michael champions a science-backed approach to holistic wellness — ensuring every program is trauma-informed, culturally humble, and measurably effective for the communities it serves.

Education B.S. Molecular & Cellular Biology · B.S. Public Health
University of California, Berkeley
Current Position Research Associate, Salk Institute for Biological Studies
