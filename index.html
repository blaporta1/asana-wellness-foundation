Who We Are

About Us

Our Mission

The Asana Wellness Foundation exists to democratize access to yoga and holistic wellness practices for underserved and economically disadvantaged communities, fostering physical health, mental resilience, and spiritual well-being as fundamental human rights rather than privileges.

We believe that every individual, regardless of socioeconomic status, deserves the transformative benefits of yoga — a practice that cultivates mind-body harmony, reduces stress, builds strength, and promotes overall wellness. Through accessible, culturally sensitive programming delivered directly within underserved communities, we break down barriers of cost, location, and social stigma that prevent vulnerable populations from accessing these life-enhancing practices.

Our Vision

We envision a world where zip code does not determine wellness outcomes — where every person in every community has the knowledge, resources, and support to cultivate physical vitality, mental clarity, and inner peace through yoga and mindful movement.

By expanding our reach, deepening our impact, and advocating for systemic change in how society values and funds preventive wellness for vulnerable populations, the Asana Wellness Foundation strives to be a catalyst for health equity and social justice — one breath, one pose, one community at a time.

Our Programs

What We Do

We bring the transformative power of yoga directly to the communities that need it most, removing every barrier between people and their wellness.

Community-Based Programming

We bring certified yoga instructors directly into low-income neighborhoods, community centers, schools, shelters, and public spaces — eliminating transportation and facility barriers.

Free & Subsidized Classes

We offer yoga instruction at no cost to participants, ensuring financial constraints never prevent someone from experiencing the healing power of yoga.

Inclusive & Adaptive Yoga

Trauma-informed · Chair yoga · Youth yoga · Veterans · Recovery · Formerly incarcerated

We tailor our programs to meet diverse populations where they are — offering specialized sessions for abuse survivors, seniors, at-risk children, veterans, formerly incarcerated individuals, and those in recovery.

Holistic Wellness Education

Breathwork · Meditation · Nutrition basics · Stress management

Beyond physical practice, we provide education on breathwork, meditation, nutrition basics, and stress management techniques that participants can integrate into daily life.

Instructor Training & Employment

We create pathways for community members to become certified yoga instructors, fostering economic opportunity while ensuring our teachers understand the lived experiences of those they serve.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These principles are the foundation of everything we do — guiding every class, every partnership, and every decision we make.

Equity & Access

Wellness is a right, not a luxury. We actively dismantle barriers that exclude marginalized communities from self-care practices.

Dignity & Respect

We honor each participant's journey, meeting them without judgment and recognizing their inherent worth.

Cultural Humility

We acknowledge yoga's ancient roots while adapting practices to be culturally relevant and respectful of diverse backgrounds and belief systems.

Community-Centered

Our programming is designed with — not for — the communities we serve, incorporating their voices, needs, and wisdom.

Sustainability

We build long-term capacity within communities rather than creating dependency, empowering individuals to become wellness ambassadors.

Evidence-Based

We ground our work in research demonstrating yoga's therapeutic benefits while remaining open to the wisdom of lived experience.
